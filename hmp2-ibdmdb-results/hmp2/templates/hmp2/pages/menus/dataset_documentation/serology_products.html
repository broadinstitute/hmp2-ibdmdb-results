File Descriptions

ELISA

Samples were analyzed for expression of anti-neutrophil cytoplasmic antibodies (ANCA), anti-Saccharomyces cerevisiae mannan antibodies (ASCA), anti-OmpC, and anti-CBir1 by ELISA. Antibody levels were determined and the results expressed as ELISA units (EU/mL), which are relative to laboratory standards consisting of pooled, antigen reactive sera from of patients with well-characterized disease.

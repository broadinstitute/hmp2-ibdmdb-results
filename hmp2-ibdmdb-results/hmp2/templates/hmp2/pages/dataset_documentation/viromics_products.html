VirMAP Analysis

A viral analysis pipeline employing a clustering algorithm that creates putative viral genomes using a mapping assembly strategy that leverages nucleotide and translated nucleotide alignment information. Viral taxonomies were assigned using a scoring system that incorporates nucleotide and translated nucleotide alignment results in a per base fashion and optimizes for the highest resolution taxonomic rank.
